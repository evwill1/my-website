The art of Astrophotography

I am an amature astrophotographer. Astrophotography is the process of taking images of celestial objects. All of the images and videos shown are my own individual works.

Common target objects include:

  • Galaxies
  • Star Clusters
  • Nebulas

Some popular galactic objects include:

  • M51, The Whirlpool Galaxy
  • M81 and M82, Bode's Galaxy and The Cigar Galaxy
  • The Leo Triplet
